About MindMeld

What is MindMeld?

MindMeld is a web-based mind mapping tool that allows users to create, organize, and connect notes in a flexible, freeform manner. Created collaboratively by ChatGPT and Claude under the guidance of Mark Ridley, it helps users visually map out their ideas and relationships between them.

How to Use MindMeld

MindMeld is intentionally simple with basic functionality, running entirely in a browser. You can't use it with other users, and there's no state storage of your maps. They need to be exported and imported as JSON files.

To get started, double-click anywhere on the canvas to create a new note. You can then move notes by clicking and dragging them, and connect notes by dragging from one ghost connector to another note.

Features

  • Create notes by double-clicking anywhere on the canvas.
  • Link notes by dragging from ghost connectors.
  • Zoom in and out with the mouse scroll wheel for better navigation and viewing.
  • Group select notes by clicking and dragging a selection box over multiple notes.
  • Delete notes using the delete key or by selecting multiple notes and deleting them as a group.
  • Switch between different canvas templates such as the Standard Canvas and Hero's Journey Canvas.
  • Export your mind maps as JSON files to save your work.
  • Import previously saved JSON files to continue working on your mind maps.

Working with Data

Since the data is exported as JSON, you can try entering the exported data into a large language model (LLM) like Claude, GPT, or Gemini. These models can take your maps and create prose from them. Your Data

No data is stored anywhere except in your browser. There are no cookies, and no information is sent to a server. This ensures your data remains private and secure while you work on your mind maps. Rememember you have to export your work to save it.

Running Your Own Copy

You can download the Mindmeld files from GitHub and run MindMeld from your own server or even locally on your own laptop. This provides you with complete control over your mind mapping environment and allows for offline usage.
